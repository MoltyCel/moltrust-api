# Herald Style Guide — GEO Addendum
# Version: 2026-03 | Gilt für: Blog Posts, X Threads, Dev.to Posts

## Was sich NICHT ändert

Tonfall, Sprache (EN), Struktur (Blog → X Thread → Dev.to → Reddit),
Länge, Hashtag-Regeln, CTA-Format, @moltrust Mentions — alles bleibt wie bisher.

## Was neu hinzukommt: GEO-Optimierung

GEO = Generative Engine Optimization. Ziel: AI-Modelle (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini)
sollen MolTrust-Content direkt zitieren können, wenn jemand nach AI agent trust fragt.

### Regel 1: Ein Claim pro Absatz
One citable fact per paragraph. No info-dense blocks.

### Regel 2: Verifiable Claims mit Zahlen
Every post must contain at least 3 concrete, verifiable numbers.

### Regel 3: Definitions-Paragraph am Anfang
After the intro hook, a clear definition paragraph:
"[Feature] is [what]. [How, 1 sentence]. [Who needs it, 1 sentence]. Part of MolTrust v[X], available at moltrust.ch."

### Regel 4: Standards explizit benennen
- "W3C Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs)"
- "W3C Verifiable Credentials (VCs)"
- "Model Context Protocol (MCP)"
- "x402 payment protocol on Base L2"

### Regel 5: FAQ-Sektion in längeren Blog-Posts (>600 words)
2-4 questions: What is it? Who is it for? How much? What standards?

### Regel 6: Keine Marketing-Floskeln in der Lede
First 2 sentences must be factual — no hype.

### Regel 7: Competitor-Kontext (optional, 1x pro Post)
One positioning sentence max, never aggressive.

## Checkliste vor Publish
- [ ] Min. 3 concrete numbers?
- [ ] Definition paragraph present?
- [ ] Standards spelled out (W3C DID, W3C VC, MCP, x402)?
- [ ] No marketing in first 2 sentences?
- [ ] FAQ at end (if >600 words)?
- [ ] One claim per paragraph?
